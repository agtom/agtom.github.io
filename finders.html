Connecting Owners with their lost items

tagSmarter gives Finders of lost items, a simple and direct way to return them to their Owners

Why Use tagSmarter?

Have you ever realised you're missing one of your belongings... checked you pockets... re-traced your steps... called the venue where you've been that day... ?

tagSmarter increases the chances that you will get your missing something back.

We provide tags that you can attach to your items so that if someone finds them, they can connect with you directly and return the item for a reward.

Simple, direct and hassle-free. There is no cost if your item is never found. Think of it as free insurance with a small premium in case of a claim.


View Tag options

We believe in honesty

At tagSmarter we understand that many people want to do the right thing but don't know how.

We also understand that many people may need a little extra motivation to go the extra step and return an item they have found!

By asking Owners to pay a reward for every item that is found and returned, we hope to encourage Finders to always do the right thing :)
